Churro Cheesecake Bar

Description

Some love churros, others love cheesecake, and some even love bars. Well here we have all of that in one to bring everyone at your family dinner together!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 packages of brown sugar and cinnamon creamcheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Orange zest (optional)
  • crescent sheets
  • Butter
  • Cajeta (Mexican Caramel)

Steps

  1. Sprinkle the bottom of a prepared pan with sugar and cinnamon.
  2. Place a crescent sheet in the pan.
  3. Make the filling, then spread it over the crescent sheet.
  4. Top with the remaining crescent sheet, then pour melted butter over the top.
  5. Sprinkle with the remaining sugar and bake until golden brown.
